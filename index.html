Understanding Safeguarding

Learn how to recognize and respond to safeguarding concerns

What is Safeguarding?

Protection

Safeguarding means protecting people's right to live safely, free from abuse and neglect.

Prevention

Taking proactive steps to prevent harm before it occurs.

How to Respond to Concerns

  • Recognize signs of abuse or neglect
  • Respond calmly and take the person seriously
  • Record all details accurately
  • Report to appropriate authorities
  • Maintain confidentiality
